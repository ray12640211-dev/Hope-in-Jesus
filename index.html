The Gospel

We have all sinned and fallen short of God’s glory. Sin separates us from God. But God, in His love, sent His Son.

  • All have sinned — Romans 3:23
  • Sin brings death — Romans 6:23
  • Jesus died and rose again — 1 Corinthians 15:3–4
  • Saved by grace through faith — Ephesians 2:8–9

“If you confess with your mouth the Lord Jesus and believe in your heart that God raised Him from the dead, you will be saved.”
Romans 10:9

Recovery Through Jesus Christ

Recovery is not behavior modification — it is transformation. Jesus Christ does not merely help us cope; He sets us free.

Freedom From Addictions

Many people are not weak — they are bound. Addiction is often the flesh seeking relief, escape, or control. Jesus Christ came to break chains, not condemn sinners.

  • Drugs & alcohol
  • Pain killers & substances
  • Pornography & sexual bondage
  • Video games & escapism

Freedom does not come by willpower alone. It comes by repentance, surrender, and walking in the Spirit.

“Therefore if the Son makes you free, you shall be free indeed.”
John 8:36

“Walk in the Spirit, and you shall not fulfill the lust of the flesh.”
Galatians 5:16

“The Spirit of the Lord is upon Me, because He has anointed Me to preach the gospel to the poor; He has sent Me to heal the brokenhearted, to proclaim liberty to the captives.”
Luke 4:18

A Prayer for Salvation

God hears sincere hearts. If this reflects your heart, pray:

“God, I need You. I confess my sin and believe Jesus died for me and rose again. Please forgive me, make me new, and lead my life from this day forward. I trust You alone. In Jesus’ name, amen.”
