The Free Gift of Salvation

Salvation is not earned. It is given by God’s grace through faith in Jesus Christ.

“For by grace you have been saved through faith, and that not of yourselves; it is the gift of God, not of works, lest anyone should boast.”
Ephesians 2:8–9

The issue is sin (separation from God) — and the answer is Jesus: crucified and risen.

  • All have sinned — Romans 3:23
  • Wages of sin is death — Romans 6:23
  • Christ died for us — Romans 5:8
  • Confess + believe He rose — Romans 10:9
Romans Road prayer Recovery through Jesus

What to do next

Come honestly. Repent (turn from sin). Believe the gospel. Surrender your life to Jesus as Lord.

“If you confess with your mouth the Lord Jesus and believe in your heart that God has raised Him from the dead, you will be saved.”
Romans 10:9

Growth is discipleship: read the Word, pray, fellowship, worship, obey — by the Holy Spirit.

“Walk in the Spirit, and you shall not fulfill the lust of the flesh.”
Galatians 5:16

Prayer of Repentance & Faith

This isn’t magic words. God looks at the heart. If this is your heart, pray:

“God, I confess that I have sinned against You. I repent and turn from my sin. I believe Jesus Christ died for my sins and rose again. Jesus, You are Lord—You alone are the living God, and I surrender my life to You. Forgive me, cleanse me, and make me new. Fill me with Your Holy Spirit and lead me from this day forward. In Jesus’ name, amen.”
